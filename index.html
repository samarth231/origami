Interesting facts about the Camel

  1. They can go for weeks without drinking water
  2. They can live for about 40 years
  3. Camel milk is among the healthiest milk in the world

Interesting facts about the Chameleon

  1. Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  2. skin crystals help them change color
  3. Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues

Interesting facts about the Cicada

  1. Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica
  2. Some cicadas could explode with fungus
  3. Cicadas can't eat, getting nutrients from tree roots
